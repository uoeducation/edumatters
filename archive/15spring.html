Could interventions be more efficient?

Interventions meant to prevent new episodes of child abuse and neglect are time-consuming and vary in their effectiveness, but a $3.5 million NIH grant led by Elizabeth Skowron will study why some interventions work better than others, leading to custom treatments that save time, money, and frustration.
